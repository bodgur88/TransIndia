Land Acquisition for Purvankara

Trans India's Landmark Partnership with Purvankara

Embarking on a journey of excellence, Trans India has been at the forefront of numerous prestigious projects in collaboration with the renowned real estate developer, Purvankara. Through relentless dedication and a commitment to quality, Trans India has undertaken various path-breaking initiatives, leaving an indelible mark on the landscape of modern living. Central to this flourishing partnership is the acquisition of 70 acres of prime land in Sarjapura, a strategic location that holds immense promise for creating a haven of luxury and convenience.

With a shared vision of crafting exceptional living spaces, Trans India's acquisition of the 70-acre land parcel for Purvankara has set the stage for a groundbreaking residential project. Drawing inspiration from the surrounding natural beauty and incorporating cutting-edge architectural designs, the team is diligently working on constructing a world-class apartment complex that epitomizes opulence and modernity. As the project gains momentum, Trans India remains firmly committed to ensuring every aspect aligns with Purvankara's unparalleled standards of quality and customer satisfaction.
