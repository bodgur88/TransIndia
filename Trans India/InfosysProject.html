Infosys Project


Transforming Dreams into Reality: Acquiring 400 Acres for Infosys SEZ

The image above represents the total are of land acquired for Infosys in Sarjapuara. In a remarkable endeavor that exemplifies Trans India's commitment to turning dreams into reality, the company successfully acquired a sprawling land expanse of 400 acres for Infosys, the renowned multinational corporation. This strategic acquisition took place during the years spanning 2005 to 2010, marking a significant milestone in the realm of real estate and land aggregation. The land's purposeful allocation for Special Economic Zone (SEZ) development underscores Trans India's dedication to fostering growth and innovation for businesses and the region at large.

With foresight and meticulous planning, Trans India secured an expansive tract of land that now serves as a foundation for Infosys' pioneering SEZ initiative. This visionary project has not only facilitated the growth of Infosys but has also contributed significantly to regional economic development and employment opportunities. Through seamless execution and unwavering dedication, Trans India ensured that the acquisition process was completed with exceptional efficiency, enabling Infosys to establish a world-class SEZ that continues to shape the future of the IT industry and elevate the overall business landscape.
